Fitness Formula Clubs

Building a cohesive web presence for a top Chicagoland healthclub.

Role: UI/UX Designer & Front End Developer
TL;DR

Design brief and a
look into the final product.

Fitness Formula Clubs (FFC) locations feature world-class facilities, personal trainers, group fitness classes, and spas in a community-focused environment.

As the resident Web Designer, I worked alongside the in-house creative director and content writers to frame and build a web design system that would boost online membership and inform prospective members of FFC’s convenient and luxurious healthclubs. The live site can be viewed here.

The Challenge

Modernizing a large-scale membership website.

My main hurdle was to develop a site that would be completely editable, and unbreakable, by other staff members. Those without working knowledge of HTML and CSS needed the ability to independently create new pages and enter content, while I needed enough room to customize the design and pull in member data with ease.

The new website also had to be built to modern standards and still play nice with FFC’s proprietary emailing systems, third-party payment platforms and external member account software.
