Just a bit
of context.

As one of the premier engineering schools in the United States,
the University of Arizona (UA) attracts prospective students from across the country and around the globe.

Following a recent schoolwide rebrand, UA's College of Engineering needed to redesign its existing website and landing pages in order to reflect a new set of branding standards.

I used this as an opportunity to refresh the aesthetics of their enrollment site and improve a noticeably cumbersome online admissions process.

Establishing a successful MVP

I worked closely with members of UA's marketing team to chose which brand updates were to take priority in the first phase of the relaunch.

We focused on using a complimentary color palette, updated type stack and a new lead capture process as these would make the biggest impact upfront and help represent the College of Engineering within the bigger UA brand.
